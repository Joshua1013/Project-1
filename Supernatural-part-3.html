Angels

Angels are created by God himself to protect heaven and earth. Then are one of the most powerful creatures on the planet earth. They can outmatch most of the creatures ever created. The only beings that can outmatch them are Leviathans and the 4 Horsemen of the Apocalypse (Death,Famine,War and Pestilence). The first angel (Castiel) appeared in Season 4 Episode 1, angels started appearing due to demons
